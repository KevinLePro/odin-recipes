My Honey Cereal Recipe

Description

My cereal recipe will usually require the Kroger's Special K flavors

Ingredients

  • Special K cereal
  • Milk
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Grab yourself a bowl, milk, and your Special K cereal
  2. Pour 2 servings of cereal in your bowl
  3. Add 1/2 cup of Honey
  4. Pour your preferable milk serving into the bowl
